GDPR-Aligned · Version 1.0 · March 2026

We collect only what we need. We store it securely. We never sell it. You can delete it — except what is permanently on the blockchain.

What We Collect
Required at signup: Full Name, Email, Country, Username, Password (bcrypt hashed — never readable).
Optional / on action: Profile photo, Sex, Skills, Portfolio files, BEP-20 wallet address (when you choose to bind).
Automatically: Session data (cleared after 24h), platform activity (jobs, challenges, contributions).
How We Store It
Personal data: PostgreSQL database with AES-256 encryption at rest. Passwords: bcrypt hash only — we cannot read your password. Files: Cloudflare R2 / S3 object storage with CDN. Session data: Redis (in-memory, auto-expires 24h). SOPHIA conversations: session only — never stored permanently.
SOPHIA & AI Data
SOPHIA conversations are processed in real-time and never stored permanently. Session context is kept in Redis for up to 24 hours for conversation continuity, then auto-deleted. SOPHIA does not have access to your wallet private keys or financial data at any time.
⚠ Important: Blockchain Data is Permanent
Your wallet address, NFT credentials (Builder/Maker/Luminary), MIC token transactions, and DAO governance votes are recorded on the BNB Smart Chain (BSC) blockchain. This data is immutable and cannot be deleted by us or by you — ever. This is the nature of public blockchain technology. Please consider this before binding your wallet.
